KeeperRL Achievement Ideas

  • Legendary Crafter: Obtain a legendary crafter through training or Slayer credits.
  • Premature Demise: Get killed before recruiting anyone.
  • Art of Looting: Obtain the Amulet of Life Saving from the Black Market (separate achievements for buying and looting it).
  • Plague Resistance: Successfully develop plague resistance for your Keeper.
  • Unlikely Companions: Have both a Ki-Rin and a Yeti in the same dungeon.
  • Dragon Menagerie: Recruit at least one dragon of each color (red, black, green, white, yellow).
  • KPI Achievements:
    • Complete the tutorial.
    • Harvest specific resource thresholds (Gold, Wood, Stone, etc.).
    • Recruit 100 Greenskins (e.g., Goblins, Trolls, Harpies).
    • Capture and convert prisoners.
    • Finish campaigns as each Keeper type.
  • Faction Specialist: Recruit creatures from multiple factions.
  • Throne Placement:
    • "King of the Hill": Place your throne on a high Z level.
    • "Underlord": Place your throne on a deep Z level.
  • Trait Collector: Have a unit with a high number of traits (e.g., 10+).
  • Low Health Victory:
    • Survive or win a game with under 5% health remaining.
    • Kill a boss while at low health.
  • ASCII Mode Master:
    • "Purist": Toggle ASCII graphics.
    • "Old School": Complete a game start-to-finish in ASCII mode.
  • Biome Explorer: Build dungeons in every biome.
  • Notable Villains:
    • Achievements for defeating specific villains.
    • Separate achievements for defeating or converting notable enemies (e.g., Minotaurs, Dragons).
  • Dwarf Achievements:
    • "No Dwarf Left Behind": Win a campaign without losing a dwarf.
    • "Rock and Stone": Beat the game as dwarves.
  • Thematic Achievements:
    • "The World's Oldest Debate": Kill all elves as dwarves.
    • "Avatar of Extinction": Create a fully equipped zombie or skeleton king.
    • "The Dark Lord": Win using only goblins as a Dark Wizard/Keeper.
  • Diplomatic Strategies:
    • "No Need for Friends": Destroy all allied factions.
    • "Diplomacy is Always an Option": Win without destroying any allies.
  • Endless Mode:
    • Survive 100 waves in Endless Mode.
    • Reach the end of Endless Mode.
  • Population Management:
    • Achievements for reaching specific minion counts (e.g., 10, 100).
    • Win a game without increasing the population cap.
  • Creative Losses:
    • "Brains Not Included": Lose 10 zombies to sunlight.
    • "It's Just a Flesh Wound": Lose all limbs on a humanoid pawn.
    • "Civil War": Lose 10 units to infighting.
  • Keeper-Specific:
    • "Double the Fun": Use the "Double Trouble" spell on your Keeper.
    • "I Wish for More Wishes!": Own multiple Djinn Lamps.
    • "Collector": Own all creature types available to the Keeper.
  • Epic Feats:
    • "Night of the Living Dead": Create 100 undead as a necromancer.
    • "Lord of Death": Recruit Death itself.
    • "Midnight Aristocracy": Evolve a vampire into a Vampire Lord.
  • Combat Focus:
    • "There Can Only Be One": Kill a main villain with your Keeper.
    • "With a Little Help From My Swarm": Achieve a 75+ bonus from the Swarmer attribute.
  • Negative Achievements:
    • "Bad Boss": Kill 10 of your own minions.
    • "Evil Rises Too Soon": Die on the first day.
